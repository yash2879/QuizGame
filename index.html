Questions

1. Read all the questions carefully.

2. Navigate through the questions easily using the white block on the bottom right of screen.

3. The options which you've selected are visible when you return to any question.

4. Marks of the quiz will be shown at the end.

5. Don't forget to have fun. (This is just a game you know!)
